Acme Support

This is a stand-in “your app”. The Foglamp HUD floats in the bottom-right and streams the agent’s execution live — steps, tool calls, tokens, and cost.

“Refund my last order and email me the receipt.”

Watch the HUD: it looks up the order, the refund fails (red flash), retries successfully, then sends the receipt.

In your own app this is just two lines: foglamp({ hud: true }) on the server and <FoglampHUD /> in the client.
